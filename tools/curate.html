Paste a Wikimedia Commons file name or URL → fetch → verify → add. Export merges into content/library.json by hand (or replace the images array wholesale). Work is saved in this browser's localStorage.

Run python3 tools/harvest.py --years 1975-2015 --per-year 6 first. These already passed the licence, resolution, exact-year and datability filters — your only job is to judge whether each makes a good puzzle. Ask two things: could a careful looker reason the year from clothes, cars, signage or tech? And is there something worth saying in the reveal? Reject anything whose medium gives the year away, and remember an anonymous everyday scene usually beats a famous one.
